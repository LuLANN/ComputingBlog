About Computing Club

What really goes on behind the screen...

Taking place in A214 every Thursday after school, people who are interested in the programming OUTSIDE of school is

gathered together to learn from the president Nishkrit Desai. He specializes in Artificial Intelligence so our topics

consists mainly of how and where AI is used in our current society. We will be talking about not only the applications of AI,

but also the thought process behind it. We will be explaining brefily how it is made, different types of AIs, and make some by ourselves.

This is a place for those who are merely interested in programming, so no previous coding experience will be needed. The purpose

of this club is to develope the growth of future "hackers, rather than "crackers".
